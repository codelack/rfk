Join the RFK Crew!

RFK is always looking to add to and expand our live broadcasts! Are you an IUK student interested in hosting a show? Got an idea? We'd love to hear it! Even if you don't have an idea, but are interested in being part of Radio Free Kokomo, we can help you get on air. We're looking for dependable, innovating, and motivated students of IUK to join the crew and add even more exciting, diverse, and interesting programming to our line up. Don't want to be on air, but are interested in the behind-the-scenes work of a radio station? We can get you in on that too! Drop us an email at , or use the form above and we'll get to work getting you involved in Radio Free Kokomo.

Submissions

RFK is always looking for great undiscovered music to add to our rotation! Are you an independent or underground musician or band looking for exposure that you can't get from mainstream radio? We'll give ya a shot! We accept and consider music of all genres, but require all submissions to meet FCC standards (lyrical content cannot contain offensive material). We don't guarantee that we'll play everything that's sent to use, but we'll definitely take a listen, and the best stuff will get some air time.

To submit, send an email with an mp3 or two attached to . Be sure to include the following information in your email:

  • Band/Artist Name
  • City/State (Country if outside the US)
  • Genre
  • Website
  • Link to Facebook (if applicable)
  • Twitter Handle (if applicable)
  • Anything else that you'd like us to know

Thanks for your interest in Radio Free Kokomo! We look forward to hearing from you!
